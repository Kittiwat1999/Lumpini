Booking Deposit Policy for Meetang Limited trading as Lumpini Thai Restaurant

(Last Updated: 28th June 2024)

Booking Deposit Requirement
  • A non-refundable deposit of £5 per person is required at the time of booking to secure your reservation.
  • This deposit will be applied to your final bill upon dining at our restaurant.
Cancellation Policy
  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours prior to the reservation time will receive a full refund of the deposit.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of the reservation time will forfeit the deposit.
No-Show Policy
  • If a booking party does not show up for their reservation without prior notice, the deposit will be forfeited.
Modifications to Reservation
  • Modifications to the reservation (e.g., changing the number of guests or the reservation time) can be made up to 24 hours before the reservation without penalty.
  • Changes made within 24 hours of the reservation will be subject to management approval and may result in forfeiture of the deposit if the new reservation does not meet the original terms.
Special Circumstances
  • We understand that emergencies happen. In exceptional cases, at the discretion of the management, deposits may be refunded or transferred to a future reservation.
Group Bookings
  • For group bookings (10 or more guests), a larger deposit may be required. Specific terms will be communicated at the time of booking.
Contact Information

To cancel or modify your reservation, please contact us directly at 01582 601788 or info@lumpini.org.

By making a reservation and paying the deposit, you agree to the terms of this booking deposit policy.
