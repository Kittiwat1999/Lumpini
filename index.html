Pad Thai

Pad Thai is Thailand’s classic noodle dish, stir-fried with rice noodles, eggs, tofu, or shrimp, and flavored with tamarind sauce, fish sauce, and a hint of sweetness. Finished with crunchy peanuts and a squeeze of lime, it’s a dish that offers the ideal mix of savory, tangy, and nutty flavors.

Tom Yum Soup

A spicy, tangy Thai soup made with shrimp, Tom Yum Soup combines lemongrass, lime leaves, and Thai chili to create a warming and aromatic broth. Often enjoyed with rice, this soup is a go-to comfort food in Thai cuisine, loved for its rich, immune-boosting herbs and perfect balance of flavors.
